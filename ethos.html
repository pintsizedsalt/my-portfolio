MCGI Activities & Memories
December 2022 Onwards
Service

DSWD Repacking

One of the most humbling days of my life. After the pandemic, unti-unti na ulit pwedeng lumabas kaya sobrang daming KKTK ang naging active. We spent hours repacking relief goods for families in need, taping boxes and sorting packs. Nakakapagod pero sobrang saya kasi nakatulong kami sa DSWD, parang nakapag-exercise na rin habang gumagawa ng mabuti. I also gained new friends that day, which meant a lot to me because isa talaga yun sa wish ko noon, na dumami friends ko sa Church para mas tumibay ako sa Iglesia at mas maging active sa mga gawain. 😊

December 2022 Onwards
Workforce

Apalit Workforce

One of my favorite gawain is the Apalit Workforce. Sobrang relaxing kasi may nature sa paligid sa Apalit, Pampanga. The boys usually help with construction sa Convention, while the girls help in the kitchen preparing free meals for the elderly, orphans, and people in the charity center. Masaya rin kasi nakakapag-bond kami during breaktime, and of course may free food din kami. Nakakapagod, pero sobrang fulfilling. 🌿😊

March 2024 Onwards
FND

Fiesta ng Dios

Fiesta ng Dios is one of the happiest events for me. Odiba puro happiest. MASAYA KASI TALAGA! May mga games kung saan kapag nanalo ka may stub kang makukuha para sa free prize sa prize booth. Marami ring booths like games at food booths with snacks na gawa ng mga kapatid, and favorite ko talaga yung food. Sobrang saya kasi libre lahat, may free medical, free haircut, at free store para sa mga nangangailangan, at kahit hindi ka kapatid sa Church puwedeng makisama. Talagang joyful at generous na event siya. 🎉😊

International Youth Convention

International Youth Convention is also one of the most exciting events for me. Maraming ganap, minsan sports fest pero mas favorite ko kapag Bible Festival ang theme. May friendly competitions like chorale, Christian dance, Bible quiz, Bible preaching, at marami pang iba from different divisions. Sobrang saya panoorin, lalo na kapag nananalo ang NCR. 😆✨
