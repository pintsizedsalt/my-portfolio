Who I Am
About Me
Sam L. Isaac
Developer · Cook · Vlog

Hi! I’m Sam, a friendly, warm, and endlessly curious soul who loves connecting with people and spreading joy. I pour my heart into everything I do, whether it’s coding interactive websites, cooking delicious meals, or creating videos, from vlogs to mukbangs to TikToks. Sharing content that makes people smile and feel seen is my kind of happiness. I thrive on supporting and loving people in thoughtful, meaningful ways, and I bring that same energy to every project I take on. Guided by my faith and fueled by curiosity, creativity, and a little mischief, I explore, create, and play with intention, whether that’s gaming, filming, or coding something new. This portfolio is a peek into my world, full of creativity, connection, and heart.

My Personality

I’m an ENFJ with a 2w3 heart. I love supporting people, spreading joy, and making meaningful connections. I’m endlessly curious, especially about tech, food, and creative projects.

I balance focus with fun. I can code all night, cook a full meal, and still make a new video for my followers. In relationships, I love deeply and wholeheartedly; I’m caring, loyal, and always ready to listen, support, and make the people I care about valued and seen.

Sometimes, I get a little bored easily. I want to try everything, but don’t always finish. I’ve dabbled in dancing, writing AUs, and so many hobbies, but school or other stuff gets in the way. I’m a bit of a jack of all trades, master of none… but hopefully, I’ll find something I’ll truly master next time.

My Family
THE ISAAC FAMILY

The Whole Crew

This is my family. We’re not perfect, and like any other family, we’ve had our share of struggles, disagreements, and difficult moments. But despite everything, we always try to be there for one another. Each of them has played a big role in shaping who I am today. They support me, guide me, and remind me why I keep going. Through every challenge, we hold on to our faith in God, and by His grace and help, we continue to stay strong as a family. No matter where life takes me, they will always be my home.
